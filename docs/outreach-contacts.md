## Sending checklist (before you send)

1. **Send from a real personal email**, not a generic one. Keep it short; make the ask tiny.
2. Lead with credibility: "built by students at Northwestern, Stanford, UCLA, Johns Hopkins,
   Cornell…" (the college belt) until you have traffic numbers.
3. One follow-up after ~5 days, then stop. Track every send in a sheet.
4. Ask for 20–30% commission (recurring if possible) when terms come up.
5. If you're under 18, a parent may need to sign the payout agreement.
